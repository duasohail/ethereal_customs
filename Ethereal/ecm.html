About Ethereal Customs
Mississauga

ECM takes pride in providing the finest quality bespoke attires for gentlemen combined with exceptional customer service. We believe that custom suits and custom shirts are not a luxury some privileged. Custom clothing is a medium to express your daily self as distinctively as you are. Most importantly, we make bespoke clothing affordable to everyone who walks through ECM doors.

The bespoke suit is nothing if not handmade. ECM combines the finest of fabrics from around the globe with excellent craftsmanship. We are deeply inspired from Tom Ford, Gianni Versace, Kiton, and Brioni and our product shows quality and craftsmanship like none other.
